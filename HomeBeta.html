Deveste Eight 8

The most powerful beast breathing 5000bhp
at 350mph on the bahn.

Price $9,000,000

Deveste 8

Both in name and in design, the Deveste Eight is clearly based on the real-life Devel Sixteen, a Dubai based concept hypercar. The shape of the headlights seems to be inspired by the Hyundai N 2025 Vision Gran Turismo.
Opposed to the butterfly doors from its real-life equivalent, the Deveste Eight uses gull-wing doors.

Lampadati Tigon

The Tigon is heavily inspired by the De Tomaso P72
seen in the body shape, front end and greenhouse area.
The headlights shape and, to lesser extend, the grille
resemble ones found in the Ultima RS. The rear of the vehicle has similarities to the rear of the Lancia New Stratos concept car.
